FAQ's

What is Fundgrazing.com all about?

  • We're an online marketplace where people like you can mint your own money.

  • Use our system to make money selling nothing at all. It's easy peasy and anyone can do it, even if you have never sold anything online before.

  • Our system is unique because it automatically generates product for you to sell. You do not have to worry about what goods you have to sell or even how or who to sell them too. Just consume items from the marketplace and more items will appear there in your name. We've eliminated all of the usual hassles of owning and operating an online store by reducing the sales process down to a single transaction.

  • The site is designed to double your money each time you consume another users item.

Who or what is Alpha?

  • Alpha is your supplier.

  • Before you can consume inventory items in our network you need to initiate your Alpha.

  • There is an Alpha for each type of inventory item.

  • There are two main inventory item types including a Beta and an a Charlie. You'll learn more about them below.

  • After you initiate an Alpha the system will automatically spawn a Beta and Charlie item for you and place it for sale in the marketplace.

Who or what is Beta?

  • Beta is an inventory item.

  • Each time you consume a Beta the system spawns a new Beta and a new Charlie for you and makes it available for sale in the marketplace. When someone consumes it you get paid, it's that simple!

Who or what is Charlie?

  • Charlie is an inventory item.

  • Each time you consume a Charlie the system spawns a new Beta and a new Charlie for you and makes it available for sale in the marketplace. When someone consumes it you get paid, it's that simple!

How it works

We reduced the process of online selling down to its simplest form.

  • You make money by selling items.

  • To accumulate items you simply consume other sellers items.

  • Each time you consume someone elses item the system spawns two new items for you to sale.

  • The more items you consume the more money you make. It's that easy!
