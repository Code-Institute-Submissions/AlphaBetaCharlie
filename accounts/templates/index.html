Welcome to the Alpha Beta Charlie Society

If you wish to earn income through the site you need to initiate your Alpha's. To do so you must go to your profile page.

You earn money on this site by consuming other users Beta and Charlie transactions. Each time you consume someone elses Beta or Charlie transaction the system rewards you with a new Beta and a new Charlie transaction that you can sell in the marketplace.

There are different denominations of ALpha's you'll see on your profile page. You have to initiate each one to consume Beta and Charlie transactions in that denomination on the marketplace. If you try and consume a Beta or Charlie transaction in the marketplace and the system recognizes you have not spawnd the Alpha denomination associated with it then the system will redirect you to your profile page so you can consume the Alpha you need.

If you are not seeing any income from the site it is because you have run out of inventory. To get new inventory you must consume Beta and Charlie transactions on the products page.
